SELECTIVE BURNING
Every Croc is randomly generated using over 200 different variables including expression, skin, eyes, headwear and much more!
The Rarity of your Croc depends on the variables it has. This is randomly determined but generally the cooler it looks, the rarer it is!
Roadmap
PHASE #1
All Croc holders will get access to a private discord. Discord rankings are determined by the number of crocs held.
PHASE #2
Exclusivity Burning: Every Croc owner who holds 2 or more crocs will be able to burn 2 crocs in exchange for a chance to get a Croc of higher Rarity or Special Ability. This is a choice that any multi-croc holder can make, but is limited to only a 2,500 supply.
PHASE #3
Show off your love of the Swamp by getting Kitted out in the Sweetest Croc Merch ever created! Holders of Special Ability Crocs will receive free Merch.
PHASE #4
Personal Swamp: Every Croc holder will get the chance to mint their own Personal Swamp for their Croc to chill in.
PHASE #5
Radioactive Crocs: Every Croc holder will be entitled to mint their very own Radioactive Croc. Careful don’t let your fish near them!
